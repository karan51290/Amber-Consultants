### Tenant Screening

- Background Check : Conduct background and credit checks to ensure the tenant meets your criteria.
- References : Verify employment and previous landlord references.

### Lease Agreement

- Draft Lease : Prepare a lease agreement that outlines the terms and conditions of the tenancy.
- Review and Sign : Review the lease with the tenant and have both parties sign it.
- Security Deposit : Collect the security deposit and first month's rent.

### Move-in Inspection

- Condition Report : Conduct a move-in inspection with the tenant and document the condition of the property.
- Sign Report : Have the tenant sign the condition report to acknowledge any pre-existing issues.

### Utility Setup

- Utility Transfer : Assist the tenant in setting up or transferring utility accounts into their name.
- Provide Contacts : Provide contact information for utility companies.

### Follow-up

- Check-in : Follow up with the tenant after a few weeks to ensure they have settled in comfortably and address any concerns.

## B. Old Tenant Exit


#### 1. Notice Period

- Tenant Notice : Ensure the tenant provides a written notice of their intent to vacate as per the lease agreement.
- Confirm Exit Date : Confirm the move-out date with the tenant and provide them with a move-out checklist.

#### 2. Inspection

- Pre-move-out Inspection : Schedule an initial inspection to identify any potential issues or damages.
- Final Inspection : Conduct a final inspection after the tenant has vacated to assess the condition of the property.

#### 3. Addressing Damages

- Damage Assessment : Document and photograph any damages beyond normal wear and tear.
- Repair Estimates : Obtain estimates for repairs and deduct the costs from the tenant's security deposit if applicable.

#### 4. Security Deposit

- Deposit Return : Process the return of the security deposit, minus any deductions for damages or unpaid rent, within the legally required timeframe.
- Itemized Statement : Provide the tenant with an itemized statement detailing any deductions.

#### 5. Transfer

- Final Bills : Ensure the tenant settles all utility bills before moving out.
- Collect Keys : Collect all keys, access cards, and any other property items from the tenant.

#### 6. Cleaning and Repairs

- Professional Cleaning : Hire professional cleaners to ensure the property is in top condition for the next tenant.
- Repairs and Maintenance : Complete any necessary repairs and maintenance tasks.

By following these processes, Amber Consultants can ensure a smooth transition for both exiting and incoming tenants, maintaining high standards of property management.
