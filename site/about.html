Welcome To

Amber Consultants

Amber Consultants looks after property for owners who can't always be there themselves - managing it day to day, buying, selling, or renting it out, fixing it up, or redesigning it from the inside out. Four services, one team, so nothing falls through the gap between them.

Our Expertise

Started in property management - grew because owners kept asking for more.

Owners who trusted us to manage their property started asking the same team to help sell it, renovate it, or redesign it - so we built out those services properly instead of just referring people elsewhere. Today that means one team handling tenants and rent, transactions, repairs, and interiors, for owners who often can't be on site to oversee any of it themselves.

Why Choose Amber Consultants?

Five reasons owners trust us with their property.

Reliability

We are committed to providing consistent and reliable services, giving you confidence that your property is in safe hands.

Transparency

Our transparent approach ensures you are always in the loop regarding the status and performance of your property.

Expertise

From lease disputes to renovation quotes to closing a sale, our team has handled the specific challenge you're facing before.

Customer-Centric Approach

We prioritize your needs and preferences, tailoring our services to meet your specific requirements.

Peace of Mind

Whatever we're doing for your property, it's handled by people who treat your investment as carefully as their own.

Our Commitment

We treat your property as carefully as if it were our own.

A property is one of the biggest investments most people make - whether you're renting it out, selling it, renovating it, or redesigning it, we treat the decision with the weight it deserves, not as one job among many on a list.

Let's get in touch.

Whether it's management, a transaction, a repair, or a redesign, tell us what your property needs and we'll take it from there.
