Interiors Designed and Built In-House

Most interior design firms design the space and outsource the build. We do both under one roof - the same team that draws up your kitchen or living room also manufactures and installs it, so what you sign off on paper is what you get.

Our factory runs on Italian machinery, which is a large part of why the finish holds up years after installation instead of chipping or warping within a season.

We work across bedrooms, kitchens, living rooms, and full homes, as well as commercial fit-outs. Keeping manufacturing in-house means we're not marking up someone else's carpentry - the savings show up in what you pay, not just in what we promise.

Materials get chosen for how a space will actually be lived in or used day to day, not just to fill a brief. That's the difference between a room that photographs well and one that still works for you a year later.

Interior Design Studio

Design that starts with how you'll use the space.

Our designers begin with a conversation about how you actually live or work in the space, not a mood board - the aesthetic direction comes after that, not before it.

Where a client already has a clear picture in mind, we build to it. Where they don't, we bring options - layouts, materials, and colour palettes that suit the space and the budget, explained plainly enough that the choice is easy to make.
